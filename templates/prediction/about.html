About Our System

The Earthquake Magnitude Prediction System represents a groundbreaking fusion of geophysical science and artificial intelligence. Developed at The University of Dodoma, our platform leverages machine learning to analyze seismic patterns and predict earthquake magnitudes with high accuracy.

Using historical seismic data from 1970 to 2025, our system identifies patterns to enable proactive preparedness, potentially saving lives and infrastructure.
