About Earthquake Magnitude Prediction System

Providing seismic data analysis and prediction since 2025

Our Mission

The Earthquake Magnitude Prediction System is dedicated to advancing the understanding and prediction of seismic activity in Tanzania and beyond. Since 2025, our platform has leveraged cutting-edge data analysis and machine learning to provide accurate earthquake predictions and visualizations, empowering communities and researchers with actionable insights.

Our system integrates real-time seismic data with advanced algorithms to generate heatmaps, time-series analyses, and statistical insights. We aim to support disaster preparedness and risk mitigation by making seismic data accessible and understandable to all.
